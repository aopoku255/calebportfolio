Meet Caleb

Caleb Agyemang-Duah is one of the most promising young voices shaping Africa’s innovation and digital future. At his early 20s, he wears many titles—tech ecosystem builder, digital transformation lead, keynote speaker, community architect, and CEO of the RealWorld Tech Innovation Center (RTIC), one of Ghana’s fastest-rising innovation hubs.

Through his work in entrepreneurship, policy advisory, and digital transformation, Caleb has become a sought-after speaker, DT expert and consultant for startups & NGOs seeking bold, youth-driven innovation. From training over 300 innovators across student and professional networks to launching high-impact pilot projects with public-sector relevance, Caleb’s work is igniting a new wave of purpose-led, tech-driven change.

You don’t need permission to build the future. You just need a reason to start.”

– Agyemang-Duah Caleb
